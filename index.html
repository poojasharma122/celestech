OUR HISTORY

Celestech began with a simple mission: to make solar observation safe and accessible for everyone. Our team collaborated with astronomical institutions worldwide, ensuring millions could experience the wonder of a solar eclipse without risk to their eyes.

  • 2015 Foundation
  • 2016 Expansion
  • 2017 New Tech
  • 2018 Global Reach
2015 The Beginning Of A Vision

Founded with a vision for safe eclipse viewing, Celestech began its journey with a commitment to protecting eyes during solar events. Our small team of astronomy enthusiasts and optical engineers set out to create the safest solar viewing products available.

In our first year, we developed proprietary lens technology that exceeded international safety standards while maintaining exceptional visual clarity.
