Who We Are

At Celestech, we are dedicated to crafting certified solar eclipse glasses in Spain. With a passion for astronomy and a commitment to safety, we empower skywatchers worldwide to experience every celestial moment with clarity and protection.

Every pair of our glasses is manufactured under the strictest European quality standards and tested in our laboratories using precision spectrophotometers to ensure full ISO & CE compliance. We incorporate safety seals and holograms on each product to guarantee authenticity, giving our customers peace of mind during every solar event.

OUR MISSION

Celestech arises in Spain as the natural evolution of a consolidated experience in the manufacture of solar eclipse glasses and safe observation products in North America. For years, our team has collaborated with internationally renowned astronomical institutions and has supplied millions of certified glasses, recognized for meeting the most demanding safety standards.

The next total eclipse in 2026 will be a historic event, partially or totally visible to millions of people on the continent. Faced with this challenge, our commitment is to become the largest European manufacturer of solar eclipse glasses, with a daily production capacity of up to 350,000 units, so that everyone can enjoy this phenomenon in a safe and certified way.

WHY EUROPEAN PRODUCTION?

Ensuring safety, authenticity, and peace of mind for your eyes.
